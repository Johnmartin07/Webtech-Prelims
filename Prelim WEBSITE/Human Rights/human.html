Human Rights 


Human Rights are norms or moral principles which describe a certain standards of human behavior, these moral principles are protected 
as legal rights in all over the world.These norms or moral principles are focus on dignity, equality, justice, and peace.Human rights 
are something to which you are entitled by virtue of being man, which means every man have human rights. Human rights are important thus 
human rights gives people freedom to choose how they live, human rights also protects people against abuse by those who are more powerful. 

Human rights are applicable all over the world. In the Philippines there are a lot of place that supports human rights just Like Barangay Mirador.
Barangay Mirador is doing their best to support human rights as their barangay comply laws that are related with human rights. Even though Barangay
Mirador is compliant with these laws, not as they wanted, there are still human rights related cases that are listed in their records.
